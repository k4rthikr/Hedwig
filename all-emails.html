You currently own the following emails:

EventName Email Manage
RetailOrderConfirmation Confirmation email on successful purchase of one or more items, all non digital. This event is only sent when orders reach COW. Most orders reach COW almost immediately after they are placed, but there are important exceptions. For example, one-click orders don't reach COW until they have past the one-click hold, and check orders don't reach COW until after funds are received. In both cases, if the order is cancelled before it reaches COW, it is never "ready for processing" so no OrderReadyForProcessing event will be published. As a side note, this message will be resent anytime something on the order changes that requires us to rerun our Prefulfillment workflow.
